Welcome to the swedish Honda vintage car club

If you like old Hondas, we would really like to see you in our club


Welcome! If you love classic Hondas — from early Civics and CRXs to other vintage models — you're among friends. We share restoration tips, host meetups, and help each other keep these cars on the road and in great shape.

Our goal is to keep classic Honda vehicles running by providing a platform where passionate owners and enthusiasts can get to know each other. We aim to preserve, restore, and enjoy older Hondas through shared knowledge, community events, and mutual support — keeping the history and spirit of Honda alive for future generations.
