Welcome to the swedish Honda vintage car club

Welcome! If you love classic Hondas — from early Civics and CRXs to other vintage models — you're among friends. We share restoration tips, host meetups, and help each other keep these cars on the road and in great shape.

Our mission is to celebrate the legacy of classic Honda vehicles by connecting passionate owners and enthusiasts. We aim to preserve, restore, and enjoy older Hondas through shared knowledge, community events, and mutual support — keeping the history and spirit of Honda alive for generations to come.
